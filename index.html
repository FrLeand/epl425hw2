About us

Grandmom's Sweet Kitchen is a cooking blog featuring everyone's beloved Grandmom Suzy.
While Grandmom's kitchen mostly smells like sweets, you can also find savory recipes as well as guides for other handcrafted goodies.

Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie



As you step into Grandmom's kitchen for the first time a zesty and tangy smell is in the air. It smells really similar to lemons and yet it smells sweet as well.
That's because Grandma's Lemon Maringue Pie was just done baking.
Featuring a fragrant flaky crust, sweet lemon filling with just the right amount of sourness and incredibly fluffy meringue.
The perfect afternoon treat along with a cup of tea or lemonade.
